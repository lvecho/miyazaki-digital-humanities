🌟 Explore the Magical World of Miyazaki

Welcome to our digital humanities project! This academic website delves into the animated world of Hayao Miyazaki. Using data analysis and visualization, we reveal the narrative structures, character development, and themes of classics like *Spirited Away*, uncovering what makes them timeless.

Through text analysis and visual methods, we reinterpret Miyazaki's animation. We analyze scripts, dialogues, and emotional flows, creating unique visualizations for deeper understanding.
